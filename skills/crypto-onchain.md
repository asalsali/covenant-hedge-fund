# On-Chain Metrics Analysis

Follow this checklist when evaluating a digital asset's on-chain health and market structure.

## Workflow Checklist

- [ ] **Supply scarcity assessment**: Calculate circulating supply / max supply ratio. Above 80% = scarce (bullish for store-of-value thesis). Below 50% = significant dilution ahead (bearish pressure from future unlocks). No max supply cap = inflationary token, evaluate emission rate.

- [ ] **Dilution risk check**: Compare market cap to fully diluted valuation (FDV). If FDV > 2x market cap, substantial tokens remain unlocked -- these represent future sell pressure. FDV/MC ratio near 1.0 = minimal dilution risk. Above 3.0 = extreme caution.

- [ ] **Volume-to-market-cap ratio**: Calculate daily volume / market cap. Above 10% = high velocity, suggests active trading or speculative churn. Below 1% = low liquidity, positions may be difficult to exit. Range 2-8% = healthy trading activity.

- [ ] **Multi-timeframe momentum alignment**: Check 7d, 30d, and 200d price changes. All positive = confirmed uptrend (high conviction). All negative = confirmed downtrend (avoid or short). Mixed = transitional, weight the longer timeframe more heavily.

- [ ] **ATH/ATL distance**: Calculate current price distance from all-time high and all-time low. Within 20% of ATH = distribution risk, potential for profit-taking. More than 80% below ATH with positive short-term momentum = potential recovery play. Near ATL with declining volume = capitulation may be incomplete.

- [ ] **Market cap rank trajectory**: Is the asset's rank rising or falling relative to the broader crypto market? Rising rank + rising price = genuine demand and relative outperformance. Rising price + falling rank = merely riding a broad market lift, not outperforming.

- [ ] **Cycle positioning**: Based on the above, classify the asset's market cycle phase:
  - **Accumulation**: price near lows, low volume, supply tightening
  - **Markup**: price rising, volume expanding, momentum aligned bullish
  - **Distribution**: price near highs, volume high but price stalling
  - **Markdown**: price falling, volume spikes on down days

- [ ] **Final synthesis**: Combine supply dynamics, momentum, and cycle position into a single signal. Weight supply scarcity and dilution risk more heavily for long-term holds. Weight momentum and volume more heavily for medium-term positions.
